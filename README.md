A clean, mobile-friendly web application designed to track scores for the popular Golf card game. No more searching for scraps of paper or dull pencils—simply enter the players and start tallying.

Live Demo: <https://colebarkley.github.io/golf-cards-scorecard/>

✨ <b>Features</b></p>
• Flexible Player Count: Supports anywhere from 3 to 12 players, making it perfect for small groups or large family gatherings.</p>
• Custom Game Length: Set your game for 9 holes, 18 holes, or as many rounds as you want to play.</p>
• Match History: Automatically saves your previous games so you can settle debates about who the reigning champion is.</p>
• Persistent Data: Uses local browser storage to keep your names and scores safe even if you accidentally refresh the page.</p>
• Responsive Design: Optimized for use on tablets and smartphones right at the card table.

🃏 <b>How to Use</b>
1. Set Up: Select the number of holes you plan to play and the number of players (up to 12).
2. Name Players: Enter the names of everyone at the table.
3. Lock & Play: Click "Start Game & Lock Names" to begin.
4. Score: Enter the scores at the end of each hole. The app handles all the math!
5. Review: Check the Match History section to see a log of all completed games.

📝 <b>About the Game</b>
"Golf" is a card game where the objective is to earn the lowest number of points over the course of nine or eighteen holes (deals). This scorecard simplifies the process by tracking totals and identifying winners automatically.
